## Full description (≤ 4000 chars)
Budget Pro helps you take control of your money with a clean, mobile-first experience:

• Smart transaction tracking (income & expenses)
• Monthly budgets by category with alerts
• Visual financial goals (savings, purchases, debts)
• Clear reports with charts (trends, categories, cashflow)
• Dark mode, installable PWA, offline support
• Notifications (goals & debts reminders) – optional

### Key features
- Fast input: add a transaction in 3 taps
- Categorization: automatic & manual
- Budgets & alerts: monitor monthly consumption
- Goals: visualize your progress & contributions
- Reports: category spend, cashflow, comparisons
- Export: Excel/CSV for accounting
- Mobile-first: designed for Android (Material 3)

### Offline experience
- The app keeps working without internet
- Goal contributions are queued and sync when reconnected

### Privacy-first
